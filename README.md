# RuneGains

**Your RuneScape progress. Stored locally. Always yours.**

RuneGains is a desktop app that tracks your RuneScape experience gains over time—while keeping all of your data on your own computer.

No accounts. No servers to rely on. Just your progress, safely stored with you.

---

## What is RuneGains?

RuneGains records your character’s stats each day and shows you exactly what you’ve gained.

You’ll be able to clearly see:

- XP gained today
- XP gained over time
- Progress across all skills
- Your long-term account growth

---

## Why RuneGains?

Other tracking tools depend on websites and servers. When they go down—or lose data—your progress can disappear.

RuneGains is different.

- Your data is stored locally on your PC
- Your progress is always available—even offline
- No accounts or logins required
- **No data is sent to any servers**
- You are in full control

If it matters to you, it should stay with you.

---

## How it works

1. Add your RuneScape character
2. RuneGains checks your public stats
3. It saves a snapshot of your progress
4. Each time you open the app, you see exactly what you’ve gained

Simple, reliable, and built to last.

## Is it safe?

Yes.

RuneGains only reads publicly available RuneScape data.
It does **not** ask for your login, password, or any private information.
**No data is ever sent to external servers.**

Your data stays on your machine—where it belongs.
